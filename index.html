What is lmbk?

lmbk is a people platform providing data-driven stress and wellness solutions in our every day lives.

Stress underlies all major illness and costs the US economy hundreds of billions each year. Founded by a team of neuroscientists, technologists, and healthcare professionals, we are a mission driven technology platform pioneering tracking solutions for workplace stress. Powered by real-time neurostress tracking, lmbk provides teams feedback to increase productivity, reduce employee burnout, and enhance wellness in your most valuable asset: your people.
